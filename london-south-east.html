FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How does working in a multi-story building environment affect my fire safety training?

Due to the complexity of high-rise evacuations, training emphasises understanding your specific floor's zoned alarm system, knowing the nearest refuge area for assisting disabled persons, and following the instructions of the Fire Warden exactly during a phased evacuation.

Are there unique site access or contractor safety induction requirements in this region?

Yes. Many sites, particularly in central London, have stringent site-specific induction requirements mandated by landlords or security. All contractors and employees accessing these sites must complete the local safety briefing in addition to company-wide training.

What special risk training is required for employees using public transport frequently in the South East?

While travel is a personal risk, training emphasises slips, trips, and falls around stations, and for those who drive, defensive driving and fatigue management training is reinforced due to heavy congestion and frequent early starts and late finishes.
