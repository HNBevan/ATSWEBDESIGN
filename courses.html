First Aid Training

Professional First Aid Training

Accomplished Training Solutions delivers comprehensive first aid training courses across the whole of the UK. All our courses are HSE compliant and meet current UK workplace legislation. Whether you need a one-day EFAW, the full FAW qualification, Paediatric First Aid, or BLS NHS Compliant training, we bring expert training directly to your workplace. Read our client testimonials or view our gallery.

Emergency First Aid at Work (EFAW)

1 Day Valid 3 Years

The Emergency First Aid at Work (EFAW) course is ideal for low-risk workplaces and provides essential first aid skills. This one-day HSE-approved course covers the fundamentals of emergency first aid response.

Course Content

  • Roles and responsibilities of a first aider
  • Assessing an incident and managing casualties
  • CPR and use of defibrillators (AED)
  • Managing an unresponsive casualty
  • Treating wounds and bleeding
  • Minor injuries including burns and scalds
  • Shock management

Who Should Attend?

Suitable for employees in lower-risk environments, those requiring basic first aid knowledge, and businesses with smaller teams. Also ideal for refresher training or those wanting fundamental first aid skills.

HSE Compliant On-Site Delivery 3 Year Validity

First Aid at Work (FAW) 3 Day

3 Days Valid 3 Years

The comprehensive First Aid at Work (FAW) course is a three-day HSE-approved qualification for workplaces. This in-depth course provides extensive knowledge and practical skills to deal with a wide range of first aid emergencies.

Course Content

  • Roles, responsibilities and health & safety legislation
  • Assessing incidents and managing emergency situations
  • CPR and AED use
  • Managing unresponsive casualties and choking
  • Wounds, bleeding, shock and minor injuries
  • Fractures, dislocations and spinal injuries
  • Chest injuries and medical emergencies
  • Anaphylaxis, heart attacks, strokes, asthma and diabetes
  • Poisoning and electrical injuries
  • Recording and reporting accidents

Who Should Attend?

Essential for designated workplace first aiders, employees in higher-risk environments (construction, manufacturing, warehouses), and businesses where the HSE requires qualified first aiders.

HSE Approved Comprehensive Practical Training

First Aid at Work Requalification

2 Days Valid 3 Years

The First Aid at Work Requalification course is designed for individuals who hold a current or recently expired FAW certificate (within 28 days). This two-day refresher updates your first aid knowledge and skills.

Course Content

  • Review of first aider roles and responsibilities
  • Updated emergency response procedures
  • CPR and AED refresher training
  • Treating casualties with various injuries and conditions
  • Latest first aid techniques and protocols
  • Practical scenario-based assessments

Who Should Attend?

For anyone who currently holds a First Aid at Work certificate that is due to expire or has expired within the last 28 days. If expired more than 28 days, the full 3-day FAW course is required.

HSE Compliant Shorter Duration Skills Update

Paediatric First Aid

2 Days Valid 3 Years

Our Paediatric First Aid course is specifically designed for those working with babies and children. This comprehensive two-day course is Ofsted compliant and meets EYFS requirements for nurseries, schools, and childcare providers.

Course Content

  • Roles and responsibilities when caring for children
  • Infant and child CPR techniques
  • Managing choking in babies and children
  • Treating wounds and bleeding in children
  • Burns, scalds and electrical injuries
  • Common childhood illnesses and allergies
  • Febrile convulsions and meningitis recognition
  • Asthma and anaphylaxis in children
  • Head and spinal injuries

Who Should Attend?

Essential for nursery staff, childminders, teachers, teaching assistants, sports coaches, and anyone working with children. Mandatory for Ofsted registered childcare providers.
