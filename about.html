Our Story

18+ Years of Excellence in Health & Safety Training

Established in 2006, Accomplished Training Solutions has been at the forefront of health and safety training across the United Kingdom for over 18 years. Based in Kent, we've grown from a local training provider to a trusted nationwide service, delivering HSE-compliant courses to businesses in every corner of the UK.

What started as a commitment to providing quality workplace safety training has evolved into a comprehensive training solution serving thousands of businesses across retail, construction, healthcare, education, hospitality, and corporate sectors. See what our clients say.

Our longevity in the industry isn't just about years in business. It's about consistent quality, unwavering commitment to compliance and a genuine passion for keeping workplaces safe across the United Kingdom.

HSE Compliance & Recognised Certifications

All our training courses are fully compliant with Health and Safety Executive (HSE) requirements and current UK legislation. We stay up to date with every regulatory change to ensure your business meets its legal obligations.

Our certifications are recognised nationwide and accepted by insurers, regulatory bodies, and during workplace inspections. Certificates are issued on the day of training. Get in touch to book your training.

With Accomplished Training Solutions, you comply with:

  • Health and Safety at Work Act 1974
  • Management of Health and Safety at Work Regulations 1999
  • Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005
  • Manual Handling Operations Regulations 1992
  • Food Safety Act 1990
