Expert Training Across the Midlands

Accomplished Training Solutions is proud to deliver professional workplace training courses throughout the Midlands. We provide on-site training at your workplace premises across the West Midlands, East Midlands, and surrounding areas including Birmingham, Leicester, Nottingham, Coventry, Wolverhampton, Derby, and beyond.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What training is most commonly required for manufacturing and logistics businesses in the Midlands?

Manufacturing and logistics businesses in the Midlands most frequently book Manual Handling, First Aid at Work, Fire Safety, Working at Heights, and COSHH Awareness training. These courses address the most common risk areas in warehouse, factory, and distribution environments. We can deliver all of these on-site at your premises on the same day or across multiple visits.

Can you accommodate training for businesses that operate shift patterns?

Yes. We understand that manufacturing, automotive, and logistics businesses across the Midlands often operate 24-hour shift patterns. We offer flexible scheduling including early morning, evening, and weekend sessions to ensure all staff can receive their training without disrupting your operations. Contact us to discuss a schedule that works for your business.

Do you provide training for the automotive sector in Coventry and the West Midlands?

Absolutely. We regularly deliver training to automotive manufacturers, suppliers, and engineering businesses across Coventry, Birmingham, and the wider West Midlands. Our courses cover the key compliance requirements for this sector including Manual Handling, Working at Heights, COSHH Awareness, Fire Safety, and First Aid. All training is delivered at your site with no need for employees to travel.

How quickly can you arrange training for a Midlands business?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours and can often arrange training within a few days of booking, subject to trainer availability. For urgent compliance requirements, please call us directly on 0800 XXX XXXX and we will do our best to accommodate your needs as quickly as possible.
