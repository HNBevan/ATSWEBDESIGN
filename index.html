Why Training Matters

Why Workplace Training Matters

Under the Health and Safety at Work Act 1974, employers have a legal duty to provide adequate training. Well-trained teams are safer, more confident and more productive.

Benefits for Employees

Greater confidence and competence in handling workplace emergencies
A safer working environment with reduced risk of injury or illness
Recognised certifications that enhance career development and employability
Peace of mind knowing how to respond in a first aid, fire or safety incident
Feeling valued, training shows employers care about staff wellbeing

Benefits for Employers

Full legal compliance with HSE requirements and UK workplace legislation
Reduced workplace accidents, incidents and associated costs
Lower insurance premiums through demonstrated due diligence
Improved staff retention, employees stay where they feel safe and supported
Audit-ready documentation and nationally recognised certificates

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What courses do you offer?

We offer a wide range of workplace training courses including First Aid (EFAW, FAW, Paediatric, BLS NHS, AED & CPR), Fire Safety (Fire Warden, Fire Marshal, Fire Safety Awareness, Fire Risk Assessment, Evacuation Chair), Food Safety (Level 1, Level 2, Allergen Awareness), Health & Safety (Manual Handling, Working at Heights, IOSH Managing Safely, COSHH, Risk Assessment), and Specialist courses (Disability Discrimination Awareness, Mental Health First Aid). We also offer fully Bespoke Training tailored to your organisation.

Where do you deliver training?

All of our training is delivered on-site at your workplace, anywhere across the UK. We come to you, saving your team travel time and keeping disruption to a minimum. We cover England, Scotland and Wales.

Are your courses HSE compliant?

Yes. All of our courses meet current UK workplace legislation requirements and are HSE compliant. First Aid courses are approved and certificates are valid for 3 years. Fire Safety courses meet the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005, and Food Safety courses are nationally accredited.

How many people can attend a course?

We train groups of all sizes at your premises. Group bookings are generally more cost effective. We will try to accommodate your needs and schedule around your working hours.

Can you create a bespoke training course for our organisation?

Absolutely. Our Bespoke Training service allows us to design a course tailored specifically to your industry, workplace environment and team requirements. Get in touch to discuss your needs and we'll put together a programme that works for you.

How quickly can you arrange training?

We aim to be flexible and responsive. In many cases we can arrange training shortly after enquiry. Contact us with your preferred dates and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
